The Ship's Quarters

Five Rooms, Five Adventures

From the keel to the crow's nest, every deck of our ship hides a different way to dine.

Main Deck · Hearty Feasts

The Galley Dining Hall

The soul of the ship. In the Galley, copper pots swing overhead, the stove never sleeps, and the long communal table welcomes every hungry sailor. Watch our chefs cook over open flame just steps from your seat — no dish leaves this kitchen without a story.

  • Open-kitchen chef's counter and communal long table
  • Signature dish: Pirate's Feast Platter for the whole crew
  • Seats up to 40 guests · family style service
Reserve the Galley
Lower Deck · Cocktails & Fine Dining

Aquarium Lounge & Bar

Descend below the waterline into a room wrapped in living ocean. A curved aquarium wall circles the lounge, filling it with slow blue light and schools of tropical fish. It is the most romantic seat on the ship — and the most requested.

  • Panoramic 180° aquarium wall with reef fish
  • Signature drink: Deep Blue Sunset cocktail
  • Perfect for dates, anniversaries, and proposals
Reserve the Lounge
Study Deck · Coffee & Pastry

The Library Café

A hush falls the moment you enter. Thousands of old volumes line the curved shelves, a spiral staircase climbs toward the charts room, and the smell of fresh coffee mixes with old paper. Order a pot of tea, pick a book, and lose track of the tide.

  • Single-origin coffee, loose-leaf teas, house pastries
  • Quiet reading nooks and a working antique map corner
  • Laptop-friendly on weekday mornings
Reserve a Nook
Top of the Mast · Night Bar

Crow's Nest Sky Bar

Climb the mast to the ship's lookout — a round room ringed with windows and open sea. By day it is the calmest perch aboard; by night it becomes an intimate bar where the horizon glows and the drinks are poured strong. Only twelve seats. Worth the climb.

  • 360° panoramic view of the harbor and open sea
  • Signature drink: Three Swords Old Fashioned
  • Open late — until 02:00 on weekends
Reserve the Nest
Open Air · Brunch & Sunshine

Lawn Deck Terrace

Yes — that is real grass on the deck of a ship. Kick off your shoes, claim a picnic table beneath the parasols, and pick a tangerine straight from our little grove. The Lawn Deck is where brunch stretches into afternoon and nobody minds.

  • Real lawn deck with tangerine tree grove
  • Weekend brunch, fresh juices, and a deck swing
  • Kid-friendly and pet-friendly every day
