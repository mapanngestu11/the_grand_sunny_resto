Our Story

Built by Dreamers

Every great ship needs a crew. Every great meal needs a story.

A Ship That Never Stopped Sailing

The Grand Sunny began as a wild idea sketched on a napkin: what if a restaurant felt less like a building and more like a voyage? Our founders — a shipwright, a chef, and a navigator with a taste for tangerines — spent three years building a vessel where every room tells a different chapter of life at sea.

Today, guests board at Pier 7 and choose their adventure: feast in the Galley, drift beneath the waves in the Aquarium Lounge, read in the Library, watch the horizon from the Crow's Nest, or laze on real grass on the Lawn Deck.

One rule has never changed since the first voyage: everyone who boards eats like family.
